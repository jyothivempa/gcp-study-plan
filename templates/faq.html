Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the GCP 45-day study plan and ACE exam preparation.

What is this 45-day study plan?

This is a comprehensive, hands-on curriculum designed to take you from zero GCP knowledge to ACE exam readiness in 45 days. Each day includes concepts, hands-on labs, and quizzes covering all exam domains.

Do I need prior GCP experience?

No! This plan is designed for absolute beginners. We start with cloud fundamentals and gradually build up to advanced topics. Basic understanding of IT concepts is helpful but not required.

How much time should I dedicate each day?

Each day requires 60-90 minutes on average. This includes reading the lesson, completing hands-on labs, and taking quizzes. Some capstone projects may require 2-3 hours.

Can I complete the plan faster or slower than 45 days?

Absolutely! The 45-day structure is a guideline. You can accelerate by doing 2 days per day, or take it slower at your own pace. The platform tracks your progress regardless of timing.

Is this curriculum enough to pass the ACE exam?

Yes! This curriculum covers all 5 exam domains with hands-on labs, 240 practice questions, and real-world scenarios. Combined with the included mock exams, you'll be well-prepared for the ACE certification.

Are practice exams included?

Yes! Days 43-44 include full-length mock exams simulating the real ACE test. Plus, every lesson has quizzes (240 questions total) to reinforce learning.

When should I schedule my ACE exam?

Schedule your exam for Day 46-50 (1-5 days after completing the plan). This gives you time for final review while keeping the material fresh. Day 45 provides a complete exam strategy guide.

What's the exam pass rate for this program?

While we don't track official statistics, this curriculum follows Google's official exam guide and includes best practices from certified professionals. Completing all lessons and labs significantly increases your chances.

How do I track my progress?

Your progress is automatically tracked as you complete lessons. The sidebar shows your completion status, and you can see your overall progress in the curriculum overview page.

Can I skip lessons or jump ahead?

Yes! While we recommend following the sequential order for optimal learning, you can navigate to any day directly from the curriculum page or sidebar. The content builds progressively, so earlier lessons provide foundation for later ones.

Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! The platform is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. However, for hands-on labs requiring Cloud Shell, we recommend using a desktop/laptop browser.

Do I need a GCP account?

Yes, you'll need a GCP account to complete hands-on labs. Google offers a $300 free credit for new users, which is more than enough for this entire curriculum.

Is the GCP Free Tier sufficient?

Yes! All labs are designed to use Free Tier eligible resources (e2-micro VMs, small storage buckets, etc.). We also include cleanup instructions to avoid unnecessary charges.

Will I incur costs completing the labs?

Minimal to zero. With the $300 free credit and Free Tier resources, most students complete the entire plan without charges. Day 37 includes a dedicated lesson on cost monitoring and optimization.

How do I get help if I'm stuck?

Each lesson includes detailed explanations and troubleshooting tips. For additional help, refer to the official GCP documentation links provided throughout the curriculum.

Is this curriculum updated regularly?

Yes! The curriculum is maintained to reflect the latest GCP services and exam updates. Major updates are released quarterly, with minor fixes and improvements ongoing.

Can I contribute or suggest improvements?

We welcome feedback! While we don't have a public contribution system yet, you can report issues or suggest improvements through our feedback channels (coming soon).
