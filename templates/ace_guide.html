Top 10 Exam Tips

  • 1. Choose Granular Roles over Primitive (Owner/Editor) unless specified.
  • 2. Prefer Managed Services (Cloud Run/SQL) over VMs for less ops.
  • 3. Know the difference between Zonal, Regional, and Global resources.
  • 4. Flow logs are for network analysis; Audit logs are for who did what.
  • 5. Preemptible VMs are for fault-tolerant batch jobs to save cost.
  • 6. Use Cloud Storage classes (Nearline/Coldline) for lifecycle cost savings.

Why Hands-on Practice Matters

Hands-on real world experience is critical to preparing for Google Cloud certification exams.

Our Associate and Professional certifications are an indicator of proficiency with Google Cloud technology. Training materials are developed to build this proficiency involving the same Job Task Analyses (JTAs) as the certification exams, ensuring focus on the same underlying job role profile and required skills.

Note: Completing training is not required to fulfill certification requirements, nor are training courses guaranteed to lead to success on an exam. The recommended resources might not by themselves be sufficient for test preparation for test takers at different points in their careers.
